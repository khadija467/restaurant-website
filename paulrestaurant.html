Our Story

Where Family Meets Flavor

We wanted to create a neighborhood restaurant that reminds you of the great food Mom cooked for you. Paul Revere's is a place where you'll discover new family and friends. The service is fast and friendly. The food is delicious. We serve breakfast all day long. And don't miss our homemade pie!
